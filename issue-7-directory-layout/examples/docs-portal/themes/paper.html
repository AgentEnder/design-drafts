tinderbox theme: paper

Reading mode

The paper theme is the default. Warm off-white background, serif body face, generous line height. Optimized for reading prose end-to-end. Code blocks sit on a slightly darker tint so they don't fight the body text for attention.

The contrast ratio is intentionally lower than a pure-black-on-white sheet. A long-form quickstart printed at #000 on #fff is exhausting after the second screen. Off-white plus near-black sits in the same comfortable zone as a paperback.
